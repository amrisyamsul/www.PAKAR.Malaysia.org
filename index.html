Promoting Integrity, Trust, and Transparency

President's Speech

"At the heart of our mission lies a vision for a society rooted in integrity-where honesty, ethical values, and justice are not merely ideals, but everyday realities. As we work to eliminate corruption in all its forms, we remain steadfast in our commitment to fostering a culture where transparency and accountability guide our actions. Our efforts focus on empowering organisations with the tools and knowledge to detect and prevent corruption, ensuring they become pillars of integrity within their communities. President

About Us

PAKAR Malaysia is a non-governmental organization committed to running programs on education and delivering information on anti-corruption, abuse of power, and good governance to every layer of society. This effort increases awareness and public confidence in Malaysia's anti-corruption initiatives.

PAKAR Malaysia also supports all efforts taken by the Malaysian Anti-Corruption Commission (SPRM) to create a society that rejects corruption and strives to make Malaysia a corruption-free country.

History

PAKAR Malaysia was approved by the Registrar of Societies (ROS) on 14th December 2022 and was officially launched by YBhg. Tan Sri Azam Bin Baki, Chief Commissioner of SPRM, on 15th March 2023 at the SPRM headquarters, Putrajaya.

PAKAR Malaysia operates from the SPRM Complex in Kuala Lumpur, Jalan Cochrane. It is led by former senior officers of the Malaysian Anti-Corruption Commission (SPRM) and is supported by experienced committee members from various fields.

Objectives

The anti-corruption organization aims to create a society with integrity, where ethical values, honesty, and justice are emphasized and practiced across all societal levels.

We strive to build integrity and knowledge within organizations on how to prevent and identify signs of corruption. We also aim to establish networks with regional and international organizations sharing similar goals.
